## Pilot Design (4 Weeks, Q2 2026)

**Partner Profile:**
- Logistics company with 100-300 warehouse workers
- Active training program with safety/productivity metrics
- Willing to share anonymized data for ROI validation

**Execution:**
- **Week 1**: 10 workers complete VR training with traditional AI (CNN baseline)
- **Week 2**: Deploy neuromorphic AI (SNN), repeat VR training, measure battery/accuracy
- **Week 3**: Comparative testing, user feedback, retention assessment
- **Week 4**: Post-training performance tracking (error rates, safety incidents)

**Deliverables:**
- Comprehensive pilot report with ROI analysis
- Battery life validation (target: 5+ hours)
- SNN accuracy vs. CNN benchmark (target: <5% delta, 90%+ accuracy)
- Go/no-go recommendation for full deployment
