## 🎯 Pilot Plan Highlights

### Strategic Opportunity

**Neuromorphic AI Integration in VR Warehouse Training**
- First VR platform to deploy Spiking Neural Networks (SNNs) at enterprise scale
- Validates 15.7x energy efficiency improvement over traditional CNNs
- Enables 6+ hour battery life on Meta Quest 3 (vs. 2-3 hour baseline)
- Measures $12K/worker/year ROI from training efficiency and safety improvements

**Market Timing (2026):**

- 43% accident reduction, 75% retention, 30% time reduction proven

---

### Pilot Design (4 Weeks, Q2 2026)

**Partner Profile:**
- Logistics company with 100-300 warehouse workers
- Active training program with safety/productivity metrics
- Executive sponsorship and data sharing agreement

**Execution:**
1. **Week 1:** Baseline testing (10 workers, CNN-based VR training)
2. **Week 2:** SNN deployment (neuromorphic AI optimization)
3. **Week 3:** Comparative analysis (SNN vs. CNN, user feedback)
4. **Week 4:** Post-training validation (error rates, safety incidents)

**Success Metrics (6 KPIs):**
- ✅ Battery life ≥5 hours (target: 6+ hours)
- ✅ SNN accuracy ≥90% (within 5% of CNN baseline)
- ✅ Training time reduction ≥50% (vs. traditional 8-hour training)
- ✅ Validated ROI ≥$6K/worker/year (target: $12K)
- ✅ Safety incident reduction ≥20% (target: 43%)
- ✅ User satisfaction ≥4.0/5.0

**Go/No-Go Decision (Week 8):**
- **6/6 metrics met → Full Deployment** (commercial contract)
- **4-5/6 metrics met → Extended Pilot** (iterate on gaps)
- **0-3/6 metrics met → Conclude** (learnings for next iteration)

## 🌟 Key Success Factors

### What Will Make This Pilot Succeed?

**1. Technical Excellence**
- Pre-validate SNN models on standard datasets before partner deployment
- Achieve ≥90% accuracy with 15.7x energy efficiency
- Maintain 90Hz VR frame rate, <20ms inference latency
- Build robust telemetry for real-time monitoring and debugging

**2. Partner Engagement**
- Secure VP-level executive sponsor before pilot launch
- Weekly status calls with transparent metrics and issue escalation
- Flexible scheduling for participant availability (multiple shifts)
- Deliver value early (share preliminary results weekly)

**3. Risk Mitigation**
- Build 20% timeline buffer for unexpected delays
- Have fallback options (hybrid CNN+SNN, backup partners)
- Conservative success thresholds (5 hours battery, not 6+)
- 2-week QA phase to catch bugs before partner deployment

**4. Data-Driven Decision Making**
- Comprehensive telemetry from Day 1 (battery, accuracy, time, errors)
- Automated data collection (PostgreSQL, Grafana, Sentry)
- Statistical rigor (t-tests, confidence intervals, control groups)
- Go/no-go framework prevents subjective deployment decisions

**5. Strategic Vision**
- 2027 Intel Loihi 2 roadmap keeps pilot aligned with long-term goals
- First-mover advantage in neuromorphic VR = 2-year technical moat
- 80% pilot success probability based on conservative targets
- $1.5M ARR by 2027 = clear commercial path post-pilot
