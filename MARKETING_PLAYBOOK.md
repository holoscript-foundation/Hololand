## Target Audience Segments

### Segment 1: Minecraft Streamers (15K-50K audience)

**Profile**:
- Age: 18-35
- Platform: YouTube, Twitch
- Audience: 15K-50K concurrent viewers
- Pain point: No monetization beyond Patreon

**Messaging**:
> "Earn while you stream. Your creativity, your economy."

**Tactics**:
- Direct outreach (personalized DMs)
- Revenue calculator ("You could earn $X/month on Hololand")
- Free tier 4 (Gold) access for 6 months
- Co-streaming event (play Hololand world together)

**Success**: Convert 100 streamers → 100K audience reach = 10M impressions

---

### Segment 2: Roblox Developers (1M developers)

**Profile**:
- Age: 16-30
- Platform: Roblox Studio
- Pain point: 30% revenue share, limited asset library

- Interview series with top earners

**Pillar 2: World Building Tutorials** (30% of content)
- Quick-start guides (10-min to first world)

**Count**: 20 influencers
**Package**:
- Free tier 4 (Gold) access
- $2,000/month stipend

**Deliverables**:
- 4 videos per month
- 8 social posts per month

## Paid Acquisition

### Paid Strategy Overview

**Budget allocation**:
- Google Ads (UAC): 40% - $200K
- TikTok ads: 30% - $150K
- Facebook/Instagram: 20% - $100K
- YouTube ads: 10% - $50K
- **Total**: $500K

### Google UAC (Universal App Campaign)

**Target**: 1M installs, $0.50 CPI
**Monthly spend**: $15K-20K
**Messaging**:
- "Create. Earn. Own your community."
- "Build worlds. Get paid."
- "The creator economy starts here."

**Creatives**:
- World building in action (GIFs)
- Earning money overlay
- Player reactions (testimonials)
- Feature showcase (AI NPCs, monetization)

### TikTok Ads

**Target**: 2-5M impressions/month, 5-10% CTR
**Monthly spend**: $10K-15K
**Ad formats**:
- 15-sec world showcases
- 30-sec creator testimonials
- Interactive polls (voting on features)

**Audiences**:
- Interests: Gaming, VR, content creation, entrepreneurship
- Age: 13-35
- Retargeting: Website visitors, app uninstallers

### Facebook/Instagram Ads

**Target**: 500K-1M impressions/month
**Monthly spend**: $7K-10K
**Ad types**:
- Image carousel (5 worlds)
- Video (30-sec and 60-sec versions)
- Testimonial videos (creator earnings)
- Retargeting (abandoned sign-ups)

**Audiences**:
- Lookalikes (similar to existing creators)
- Interest-based (gaming, entrepreneurship)
- Retargeting (website visitors)

### YouTube Pre-Roll

**Target**: 500K-1M impressions/month
**Monthly spend**: $3K-5K
**Placements**:
- Gaming channels (Minecraft, Roblox, Fortnite)
- Creators' channels (Linus Tech Tips, MKBHD)
- VR channels (Beat Saber, Half-Life Alyx reviews)

**Creative**: 6-sec bumper + 30-sec main ad
