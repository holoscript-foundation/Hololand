## 🎨 Zone Design Principles

### 1. Distinct Purpose
Each zone must serve a unique function:
- Central Plaza = Social hub & welcome
- Physics Playground = Science & experimentation
- Builder Sandbox = Collaborative creation
- Market District = Commerce & shopping
- Arena = Competition & games
- Library = Education & knowledge
- Concert Hall = Events & performances
- Creative Studio = Art & expression

### 2. Interconnected
- All zones accessible via portals from Central Plaza
- Every zone has portal back to Central Plaza
- Seamless navigation, no loading screens
- ONE persistent world

### 3. Social by Default
- All zones multiplayer
- NPCs for guidance
- Collaborative features
- Shared experiences

### 4. Scalable
- Zones can be expanded
- New areas can be added
- User-created content integration
- Dynamic content loading

## 📐 Zone Specifications

### Central Plaza
**Purpose**: Main social hub & welcome center
**Key Areas**:
- Spawn/Welcome area (tutorial orbs, guide NPC)
- Social lounge (seating, hangout)
- Team coordination (4 team zones)
- Dance floor
- Portal plaza (access to all zones)

**Capacity**: 200 players
**Why**: Entry point, always busy, needs high capacity

### Physics Playground
**Purpose**: Science experiments & gravity fun
**Key Features**:
- Stacking blocks
- Bouncy balls
- Gravity zones
- Ramps
- Interactive physics

**Capacity**: 20 players
**Why**: Physics-heavy, keep count low for performance

### Builder Sandbox
**Purpose**: Collaborative 3D creation
**Key Features**:
- 4 team building platforms
- Example primitives
- Material palette
- Tool stations
- Real-time collaboration

**Capacity**: 30 players
**Why**: Creative teams, moderate capacity

### Market District
**Purpose**: Commerce & shopping hub
**Key Features**:
- Multiple vendor stalls
- Product displays
- Checkout areas
- Merchant NPCs
- Shopping carts

**Capacity**: 50 players
**Why**: Shopping district, needs space for browsing

### Arena
**Purpose**: Competitive games & PvP
**Key Features**:
- Combat arena
- Spectator seating
- Leaderboards
- Game modes
- Tournament system

**Capacity**: 40 players (20 active + 20 spectators)
**Why**: Competitive matches with audience

### Library
**Purpose**: Education & knowledge center
**Key Features**:
- Exhibit halls
- Interactive displays
- Learning stations
- Curator NPCs
- Reading areas

**Capacity**: 60 players
**Why**: Educational space, quiet exploration

### Concert Hall
**Purpose**: Live events & performances
**Key Features**:
- Stage
- Seating/standing area
- Lighting system
- Audio system
- Performer tools

**Capacity**: 100 players
**Why**: Live events need large audience

### Creative Studio
**Purpose**: Art gallery & performance space
**Key Features**:
- Gallery walls
- Art displays
- Performance stage
- Curator NPC
- Artist tools

**Capacity**: 40 players
**Why**: Intimate art viewing experience

## 🎯 Success Criteria

A zone is "complete" when it has:
- ✅ Distinct, non-overlapping purpose
- ✅ HoloScript composition
- ✅ JSON manifest
- ✅ Registered in ZoneRegistry
- ✅ NPC guide with dialogues
- ✅ Portal back to Central Plaza
- ✅ Multiplayer spawn points
- ✅ Appropriate capacity limit
- ✅ Unique features/gameplay
