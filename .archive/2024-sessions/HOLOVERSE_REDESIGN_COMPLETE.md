## 🎨 Zone Design Principles

### 1. Distinct Purpose
Every zone must be **meaningfully different**:
- Central Plaza = Social & welcome
- Physics Playground = Science
- Builder Sandbox = Creation
- Market District = Commerce
- Arena = Competition
- Library = Education
- Concert Hall = Events
- Creative Studio = Art

**No overlap, no redundancy**

### 2. ONE World Architecture
- All zones connect via Central Plaza
- Portal-based navigation
- Persistent, shared state
- Seamless transitions

### 3. HoloScript-First
- Zones defined in HoloScript
- Not compiled to Unity/Unreal
- HoloScript IS the runtime
- Direct loading into world

## 💡 Key Insights

### What We Learned

1. **Vision Before Implementation**
   - Don't convert examples blindly
   - Design the world first
   - Each zone must earn its place

2. **Consolidation > Duplication**
   - 1 great zone > 3 mediocre zones
   - Merge overlapping purposes
   - Eliminate redundancy

3. **Purpose-Driven Design**
   - Every zone answers: "Why does this exist?"
   - Distinct value proposition
   - Fits ecosystem

4. **HoloScript Paradigm**
   - Not a compiler, a runtime
   - Zones load directly
   - ONE persistent world
