**Consumer Pain** (same as original)

- 50K+ organizations displaced when Mozilla killed Hubs

- No self-service white-label VR platform with transparent pricing

**Why Hubs Failed** (NEW):
- ❌ No monetization model (free-forever killed sustainability)
- ❌ Organizational misalignment (side project, not core business)
- ❌ Blank canvas UX (high friction, low engagement)
- ❌ No creator economy (no marketplace, no royalties)

**Critical Insight** (NEW): Users migrated to FrameVR, Spatial, CYZY SPACE → **Market demand validated**, execution was the issue.

**Why Changed**:
- Research validated enterprise white-label demand (museums, universities actively use VR platforms)
- Mozilla Hubs failure analysis proved market exists but competitors have execution gaps
- Added "why Hubs failed" to position Hololand as learning from mistakes
