# White-Label Customer Interview Scripts

**Purpose**: Validate enterprise demand for white-label VR platform
**Target**: 5 interviews (2 museums, 2 universities, 1 agency)
**Timeline**: Days 4-6
**Incentive**: $50 Amazon gift card per interview
**Duration**: 20 minutes per interview

---

## 📋 Pre-Interview Checklist

### Before Each Interview
- [ ] Schedule 30-minute block (20 minutes interview + 10 minutes buffer)
- [ ] Send Zoom calendar invite with interview agenda
- [ ] Prepare recording setup (Zoom cloud recording OR manual notes)
- [ ] Have Amazon gift card email ready to send
- [ ] Review interviewee's background (LinkedIn, org website, past VR projects)

### Opening (First 2 Minutes)
"Hi [Name], thanks so much for taking the time to chat! I'm [Your Name], founder of Hololand. We're building a white-label VR platform for museums and universities.

This interview will take about 20 minutes. I'd love to hear about your VR platform needs and experience. There are no right or wrong answers - I'm just trying to understand what organizations like yours need.

As a thank you, I'll send you a $50 Amazon gift card after the call.

Is it okay if I record this for my notes? [If yes, start recording. If no, take detailed written notes.]

Great! Let's get started."
