## The Problem (Validated by Mozilla Hubs Shutdown)

**Consumer Pain**: VR creation requires expensive tools (Unity, Unreal) with steep learning curves
- 95% of VR users never create content due to complexity
- Current time-to-first-world: **15+ minutes**
- 70% of creators prefer starting from templates, not blank canvas

**Enterprise Gap** (Created by Mozilla Hubs Shutdown, May 2024):
- 50K+ organizations displaced when Mozilla killed Hubs
- Existing alternatives (Spatial.io, FrameVR) have opaque "Contact Sales" pricing
- No self-service white-label VR platform with transparent pricing
- No creator economy (template marketplaces don't exist in VR)

**Why Hubs Failed** (Research-Backed):
- ❌ No monetization model (free-forever killed sustainability)
- ❌ Organizational misalignment (side project, not core business)
- ❌ Blank canvas UX (high friction, low engagement)
- ❌ No creator economy (no marketplace, no royalties)

**Critical Insight**: Users migrated to FrameVR, Spatial, CYZY SPACE → **Market demand validated**, execution was the issue.

- **Gaming**: Boss Arena, RPG Village
- **Entertainment**: Art Gallery, Concert Hall

**Key Innovation: HoloScript + AI Generation**
```holoscript

  position: [0, 0.75, 0]
  size: [2, 0.1, 1]
```
