## 🎲 Risk Mitigation

### Risk 1: Token Price Volatility
- **Mitigation**: Dual pricing (USD + $BRIAN), auto-convert to stablecoin
- **Impact**: Low (creators choose USD for stability)

### Risk 2: Regulatory Uncertainty (SEC)
- **Mitigation**: Utility-first token (not security), legal review, US compliance
- **Impact**: Medium (monitor regulations, pivot to NFT-only if needed)

### Risk 3: Low Adoption
- **Mitigation**: Incentivize early adopters (airdrop, staking), make USD default
- **Impact**: Low (token is optional layer, not core business)

### Risk 4: Smart Contract Bugs
- **Mitigation**: Audit by Certik/OpenZeppelin, bug bounty program
- **Impact**: Medium (requires $10-20K audit cost)
