## Onboarding Philosophy

### Core Principle

**"From zero to hero in 2 hours"**

Every creator should:
- Create their first world (10 minutes)
- Earn their first payment (within 30 days)
- Reach 1K visitors (within 60 days)
- Become profitable (within 90 days)

### Learning Science Approach

**Gall's Law**: A complex system that works is invariably found to have evolved from a simple system that worked.

**Onboarding strategy**:
```
Day 1: Simple world creation (30 min)
Day 2-3: First monetization (shop setup)
Day 4-7: Marketing basics (how to get players)
Week 2: Advanced features (interactions, physics)
Week 3-4: Scaling strategies (multiple worlds)
Month 2: Specialization (choose focus: game, shop, education)
```

## Onboarding Paths

### Path 1: The Quick Start (30 minutes)

**For**: Impatient creators, visual learners

**Steps**:
1. Sign up (email or OAuth)
2. Watch 2-min welcome video
3. Follow 5-minute world creation tutorial (interactive)
4. Create first world using template
5. Deploy and get shareable link
6. Share on Discord/social

**Outcome**: Deployed world, 1-5 visitors, sense of accomplishment

---

### Path 2: The Guided Tour (2 hours)

**For**: Serious creators, learning-focused

**Steps**:

**Segment 1: Foundation (30 min)**
1. Welcome sequence (5 min)
   - Watch: What is Hololand (2 min)
   - Watch: Creator potential (revenue data)
   - Choose your path (game vs shop vs social)

2. Platform orientation (5 min)
   - Tour: Dashboard walkthrough
   - Tour: No-code builder interface
   - Tour: Publishing & deployment

3. Your first world (20 min)
   - Guided: Create from template (10 min)
   - Guided: Customize (5 min)
   - Guided: Deploy (5 min)

**Segment 2: Monetization (30 min)**
1. Understanding revenue (5 min)
   - Explainer: How creators earn
   - Explainer: Revenue splits by tier
   - Explainer: Payout process

2. Setting up shop (15 min)
   - Tutorial: Add in-world shop (5 min)
   - Tutorial: Configure items (5 min)
   - Tutorial: Set prices (5 min)

3. Marketing 101 (10 min)
   - Tutorial: Share world link
   - Tutorial: Get QR code
   - Tutorial: Post to Discord

**Segment 3: Growth (30 min)**
1. Analytics basics (10 min)
   - Tour: Creator dashboard
   - Explainer: Reading analytics
   - Explainer: Key metrics (visits, revenue)

2. Community engagement (10 min)
   - Tutorial: Join Discord
   - Tutorial: Share in showcase channel
   - Tutorial: Engage with other creators

3. Next steps (10 min)
   - Checklist: First month milestones
   - Goal-setting: Your 90-day plan
   - Support: How to get help

**Total time**: ~2 hours
**Outcome**: Deployed world with shop, marketing plan, engaged with community

## Interactive Tutorials

### Tutorial Format

**Each tutorial has**:
1. **Intro video** (1-3 min): What you'll learn
2. **Interactive steps**: Do it yourself in editor
3. **Guided walkthrough**: Coach shows how
4. **Interactive challenge**: Try it yourself
5. **Validation**: Did you do it right?
6. **Celebration**: Success message + next step

### Core Tutorials (12)

#### Tier 1: Foundations (4 tutorials, 30-45 min total)

**Tutorial 1.1: Create Your First World** (10 min)
```
1. Watch intro (2 min)
   "Let's create your first world in 10 minutes"
   
2. Interactive step (3 min)
   "Choose a template"
   └─ Interactive: Click template
   
3. Interactive step (3 min)
   "Name your world"
   └─ Interactive: Type name, see it update in editor
```

## Mentorship System

### Mentorship Matching

**How it works**:
1. Complete onboarding path → eligible for mentorship
2. Answer profile questions:
   - What's your goal? ($ earned, visit count, etc.)
   - What's your experience? (beginner, intermediate, advanced)
   - What's your focus? (games, shops, education, etc.)
   - Time commitment? (5 hrs/week, 10 hrs/week, etc.)

3. Get matched with mentor:
   - Mentor is creator 2-3 tiers above you
   - Mentor has 4.5+ rating
   - Mentor has volunteered
   - Match based on focus area

4. Mentorship period:
   - 12 weeks (3 months)
   - 1 hour/week (structured call)
   - Async messaging (Slack/Discord)
   - Goal: Help mentee reach next milestone

### Mentorship Benefits

**For mentees**:
- 1-on-1 guidance
- Accountability & motivation
- Direct feedback on worlds
- Introductions to other creators
- $50 discount on premium assets

**For mentors**:
- Recognition (badge, leaderboard)
- $100/mentee (paid for by Hololand)
- Tax deduction (mentorship counts as community service)
- Networking opportunities
- Featured in "Mentor Spotlight"

### Mentorship Outcomes

**Success metrics**:
- Mentee creates 2+ new worlds
- Mentee reaches earnings milestone
- Mentee satisfaction ≥4.5/5
- Mentee stays active 90+ days post-mentorship

**Target**: 70% of mentees advance to next tier within 6 months
