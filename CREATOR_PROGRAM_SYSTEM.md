## Program Overview

### Vision

Transform creators into entrepreneurs. Hololand provides:
- **Lowest revenue split in VR gaming** (70% to creators vs 30% to platform)
- **Direct monetization** (creators keep 100% of direct sales)
- **Predictable income** (monthly stipends for tiers)
- **Growth incentives** (bonus revenue share at milestones)
- **Holistic support** (tools, marketing, education)

### Target Creators

```
Tier 1: Hobbyists (0 - 100 monthly earnings)
        ├─ Casual world builders
        ├─ Part-time creators
        └─ First-time developers

Tier 2: Professional (100 - 1,000 monthly earnings)
        ├─ Full-time creators
        ├─ Studio teams
        └─ Specialized talent (artists, coders)

Tier 3: Enterprise (1,000+ monthly earnings)
        ├─ Large studios
        ├─ Brands
        └─ Educational institutions
```

## Creator Tiers

### Tier 1: Founding Creator

**Entry Requirements**:
- No minimum earnings
- No approval needed (self-service signup)
- Available for first 1,000 creators globally

**Benefits**:
- 70% revenue share (world sales + in-world purchases)
- $0 monthly cost
- Access to basic templates
- Basic analytics
- Community forum access
- Monthly Hololand creator digest

**Commitment**:
- Publish at least 1 world
- Update world at least quarterly
- Follow content guidelines

**Revenue Structure**:
```
Creator Earning: $100 in world sales
Platform Revenue: $100 × 30% = $30
Creator Payout: $100 × 70% = $70

Minimum payout threshold: $10/month
```

**Duration**: Unlimited until graduation to Bronze tier

**Who This Fits**:
- First-time builders testing the platform
- Hobbyists building for fun
- Students learning world building

---

### Tier 2: Bronze Creator

**Entry Requirements**:
- Earned $100+ lifetime revenue, OR
- Created 3+ published worlds, OR
- 1,000+ visits to any world

**Benefits**:

- Monthly creator meetups (virtual)

**Tools Included**:

**Revenue Structure**:

**Earnings Timeline**:

## Revenue Sharing Model

### Item Sales

**Creator in-world shop** (clothing, accessories, weapons, etc.):

```
Player purchases item for $4.99
  │
  ├─ Platform: $1.50 (30% processing fee)
  ├─ Creator: $3.49 (70% gross revenue)
  └─ Tax reserve: auto-calculated
```

**Revenue share by tier**:
| Tier | Base | At 100K visits | At 500K visits | At 1M visits |
|------|------|---|---|---|
| Founding | 70% | 70% | 70% | 72% |
| Bronze | 70% | 75% | 75% | 77% |
| Silver | 70% | 78% | 80% | 82% |
| Gold | 75% | 80% | 82% | 85% |

### Sponsorships & Advertising

**Sponsored content** (brands paying for placement):

```
Brand pays: $1,000 for sponsorship
  │
  ├─ Platform: $500 (50% platform cut)
  ├─ Creator: $500 (50% to creator)
  └─ Hololand sponsor fund: auto-set-aside
```

**Minimum sponsorship tiers**:
- Tier 1 worlds: $50 - $500
- Tier 2 worlds: $500 - $2,000
- Tier 3 worlds: $2,000 - $10,000
- Tier 4 worlds: $5,000 - $50,000+

### Entry Fees

**World entry fee** (charge players to enter world):

```
Player pays: $2.99 to enter world
  │
  ├─ Creator: $2.09 (70%)
  ├─ Platform: $0.90 (30%)
  └─ Tax: auto-calculated
```

**Best practice**: Use for premium experiences (tournaments, exclusive content)

### Subscription Revenue

**Recurring subscriptions** (optional):

```
Player subscribes: $4.99/month
  │
  ├─ Creator: $3.49/month (70%)
  ├─ Platform: $1.50/month (30%)
  └─ Annual value: $41.88 creator, $18 platform
```

**Subscription use cases**:
- Premium world access
- Season passes
- VIP cosmetics
- Battle pass equivalents

## Anti-Fraud Mechanisms

### Payment Verification

**Per-transaction checks**:
- Duplicate purchase detection
- Geographic velocity checks (can't buy from 2 countries in 10 seconds)
- Device fingerprinting
- Email verification for large orders

### Revenue Anomaly Detection

**Automated alerts**:
- Revenue spike > 500% in 1 day → manual review
- Revenue from same IP address > 100 transactions → flag
- Same payment method > 50 transactions in 1 hour → block

### Appeal Process

**If world is flagged**:
1. Immediate notification to creator
2. 7-day grace period (earnings held)
3. Creator can explain/defend
4. Hololand team reviews
5. Decision within 10 days
6. Payment released or retained based on findings

### Tax Compliance

**Automatic**:
- 1099 generation (US creators)
- Tax reserve (15% of earnings)
- W-9 collection
- Annual tax reporting
