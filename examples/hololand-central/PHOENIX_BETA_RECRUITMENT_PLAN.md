### Step 4: VR Twin Creation (3-5 days)

**Process**:

1. **Day 1: Asset Collection**
   - Download all photos from onboarding call
   - Research business online (Google Maps 360, Yelp photos, Instagram)
   - Identify key features to model

2. **Day 2-3: HoloScript Development**
   - Write HoloScript composition for VR twin
   - Model interior layout (1:1 scale)
   - Add interactive elements (menu boards, product displays, etc.)
   - Integrate real-time data (hours, weather if outdoor seating)

3. **Day 4: Compilation & Testing**
   - Compile to WebXR (browser VR)
   - Internal QA testing
   - Fix any bugs/issues

4. **Day 5: Business Preview & Approval**
   - Send preview link to business owner
   - Gather feedback
   - Make minor adjustments
   - Get final approval

**Example HoloScript** (Coffee Shop):

```holoscript
composition "PhoenixCoffeeRoastery_VRR" @vrr_twin @geo_sync {
```

## VRR Twin Creation

### Technical Architecture

**Tools Used**:
1. **HoloScript** - Declarative 3D language for VRR composition
2. **Photogrammetry** - If available, use iPhone LiDAR scan for precision
3. **Manual Modeling** - Blender/Unity for custom assets
4. **Real-Time APIs** - Square POS (menu), OpenWeatherMap (weather), Google Calendar (events)

**Quality Standards**:
- **Accuracy**: 1:1 scale replica (within 5% margin of error)
- **Performance**: 60 FPS on mid-range devices
- **Interactivity**: Minimum 5 interactive elements per twin
- **Synchronization**: Real-time data updates (menu, hours, weather)

## Risk Mitigation

### Potential Risks

**Risk 1: Low Redemption Rates**
- **Mitigation**: Offer high-value coupons (20%+ off), make quests easy (10-15 min)
- **Contingency**: Adjust coupon values mid-pilot, extend pilot by 1 week

**Risk 2: Technical Issues (Square POS integration)**
- **Mitigation**: Thorough testing before launch, dedicated support channel
- **Contingency**: Manual redemption tracking if webhook fails

**Risk 3: Business Drops Out Mid-Pilot**
- **Mitigation**: Over-recruit (target 12 businesses, expect 2 dropouts)
- **Contingency**: Replace with backup business from waitlist

**Risk 4: Poor VRR Twin Quality**
- **Mitigation**: Business preview + approval process before launch
- **Contingency**: Rapid iteration based on feedback

**Risk 5: Low Player Engagement**
- **Mitigation**: Partner with local influencers for promotion
- **Contingency**: Paid Instagram ads targeting Phoenix users ($500 budget)
